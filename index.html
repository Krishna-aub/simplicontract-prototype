Wholesale Supply Agreement
Contract Ref: C-1260  ·  Date:

This Wholesale Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date set forth above, by and between:


Flipkart India Private Limited, a company incorporated under the laws of India, having its registered office at Buildings Alyssa, Begonia & Clover, Embassy Tech Village, Outer Ring Road, Bengaluru – 560 103, Karnataka, India ("Company" or "Flipkart"); and


[ Counterparty Name ], a company incorporated under the laws of [ Country ], having its registered office at [ Counterparty Address ] ("Vendor").


Company and Vendor are each referred to herein as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties".

1. Definitions and Interpretation

In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed to them below:

"Goods" means the products, merchandise, or commodities supplied by the Vendor to the Company as specified in the applicable Purchase Order.

"Purchase Order" or "PO" means a written order issued by the Company to the Vendor specifying the Goods, quantities, delivery schedule, and applicable pricing.

"Delivery Location" means the fulfilment centre, warehouse, or other facility designated by the Company in each Purchase Order.

"Defective Goods" means any Goods that do not conform to the specifications, quality standards, or warranties set out in this Agreement or the applicable PO.

2. Term

This Agreement shall commence on [ Start Date ] and shall remain in full force and effect until [ End Date ], unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof. The Agreement may be renewed by mutual written consent for successive periods of twelve (12) months.

3. Supply of Goods

3.1  The Vendor agrees to supply the Goods to the Company in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement and any Purchase Orders issued hereunder.

3.2  Each Purchase Order shall constitute a binding contract between the Parties for the supply of the specified Goods, and the Vendor shall confirm acceptance of each PO within forty-eight (48) hours of receipt.

3.3  The Vendor shall ensure that all Goods conform to the descriptions, specifications, and quality standards set forth in the applicable PO and as required by applicable law.

4. Pricing and Payment Terms

4.1  The price for the Goods shall be as agreed in the applicable Purchase Order. All prices are inclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise stated.

4.2  The Company shall make payment within [ Payment Terms ] from the date of receipt of a valid tax invoice, provided the Goods have been received in satisfactory condition.

4.3  The total contract value shall not exceed [ Contract Value ] during the Term, unless mutually agreed in writing.

5. Delivery and Risk

5.1  The Vendor shall deliver the Goods to the Delivery Location on or before the delivery date specified in the applicable Purchase Order.

5.2  Risk of loss or damage to the Goods shall pass to the Company upon receipt and acceptance at the Delivery Location. Title shall vest upon full payment.

6. Warranties and Quality Assurance

The Vendor represents and warrants that all Goods: (a) are of merchantable quality and fit for purpose; (b) conform to all applicable specifications and legal requirements; (c) are free from defects in materials and workmanship; and (d) do not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

7. Intellectual Property

All intellectual property rights in the Goods shall remain vested in the Vendor unless expressly assigned in writing. The Vendor grants the Company a non-exclusive, royalty-free licence to use such intellectual property solely for marketing, distributing, and selling the Goods during the Term.

8. Confidentiality

Each Party shall keep confidential all non-public business, technical, financial, or other information disclosed by the other Party. This obligation survives termination for three (3) years.

9. Termination

9.1  Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience by providing thirty (30) days' prior written notice.

9.2  Either Party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other Party commits a material breach unremedied within fifteen (15) days of written notice.

9.3  Renewal type: [ Renewal Type ]. Opt-out notice: [ Opt-out Period ] days.

10. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Any unresolved dispute shall be referred to binding arbitration in Bengaluru, Karnataka, under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
